    Having lived in many cities and traveled the world, with a prior career with Delta Airlines, Abbie is happy to call Atlanta home. Currently living in Virginia Highland, Abbie has lived both the city and suburban life. This experience to so many diverse areas of Atlanta enables her to pass this knowledge onto her clients when making one of the most important decisions in their lives.

    Prior to her professional career in Real Estate, Abbie studied business at Georgia Southern University and Clayton State. All while working an internship for one of the highest volume residential closing attorneys in Atlanta. An opportunity with Delta Airlines presented her with a move to New York City where she lived for 6 years and gained intimate experience in customer service, marketing and developed a true passion for travel and working with people. After moving back to Atlanta she managed the marketing department for the same Closing attorneys she once interned for. She developed strong relationships with so many Realtors through the closing process she then decided to pursue her own career selling Real Estate.

    In 2006 Abbie parlayed all her knowledge, experience and her passion for helping people into becoming a Realtor! Abbie’s unique experience with both the selling and closing process provides additional advantages and service to her clients. She takes pride in the “personalized service” she delivers during the journey of buying and selling real estate. Joining the Intown Advisors team, and Julian Rather, was a no brainer. As she truly recognizes the benefit of a team working for the best interest of the client. Abbie genuinely loves what she does and helping others and she hopes it shows in every aspect of the Real Estate process.
